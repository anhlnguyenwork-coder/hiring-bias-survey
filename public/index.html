Candidate Evaluation Study

You will review 8 job applicants one at a time for various analyst and professional roles. Each applicant has two steps:

  • Step 1: Credentials. Review the applicant's background and rate their qualifications.
  • Step 2: Writing sample. Read their cover letter and answer questions. Credentials will not be visible during this step.

Please read each section carefully before moving on.

ⓘ  There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in your genuine professional judgment. This survey takes approximately 20 minutes.
